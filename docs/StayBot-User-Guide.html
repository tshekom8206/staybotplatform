1. Introduction

  • Purpose: How to operate StayBot for hotel operations, communication, and guest services.
  • Audience: Admins and operations staff.
  • Prerequisites: Login URL, user account, role permissions.

Quick Tips for New Users

  • Navigation is Easy: Everything you need is in the sidebar on the left. Click any section to open it.
  • Search is Your Friend: Use the search bar at the top to quickly find guests, tasks, or conversations.
  • Don't Worry About Mistakes: Most actions can be undone or edited. If you're unsure, ask your supervisor.
  • Conversations First: When a guest messages, always check "Conversations ‚Üí Active" to respond.
  • Create Tasks for Everything: If something needs to be done (cleaning, repairs, deliveries), create a task so nothing gets forgotten.
  • Use Templates: For common messages (welcome, checkout, pool hours), use templates instead of typing the same thing over and over.
  • Check Your Dashboard Daily: The main dashboard shows important numbers like pending tasks and guest satisfaction.
  • Export Reports: If your manager asks for data, look for the "Export" button on any report page.

Understanding Guest-Facing vs Staff-Facing Features

Important: StayBot has two sides - what guests see and what you (staff) see.

What Guests See (Guest-Facing):

  • WhatsApp/Messaging Interface: Guests message you through WhatsApp or other chat apps
  • Service Menu: Guests can browse and request services (room service, housekeeping, concierge)
  • FAQs: Automated answers to common questions (pool hours, WiFi password, checkout time)
  • Welcome Messages: Automated greetings when they check in
  • Broadcast Messages: Announcements you send to multiple guests

Guests never see your staff dashboard, tasks, or internal notes.

What Staff See (Staff-Facing - This System):

  • Conversations Dashboard: All guest messages in one place
  • Task Management: Internal tasks for housekeeping, maintenance, front desk
  • Guest Profiles: Booking details, history, preferences
  • Analytics & Reports: Performance metrics, satisfaction scores
  • Configuration: System settings, services, templates, staff management
  • Business Rules: Automation and workflow settings

This is your "behind-the-scenes" control panel. Guests only see their chat interface.

How They Connect:

  • When a guest messages you ‚Üí It appears in Conversations ‚Üí Active
  • When you reply ‚Üí Guest receives it in their WhatsApp/chat app
  • When you create a task ‚Üí Only staff see it (guests don't know about internal tasks)
  • When you send a broadcast ‚Üí Guests receive it as a regular message

13. Common Questions & Troubleshooting

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I know if a guest has messaged me?

A: Look for a notification badge (red number) on the "Conversations" menu item in the sidebar. Click it to see new messages.

Q: Can I delete a task by mistake?

A: Most systems require confirmation before deleting. If you accidentally delete something, contact your supervisor immediately - they may be able to restore it.

Q: What's the difference between "Priority: High" and "Priority: Medium"?

A:

  • High Priority: Urgent issues affecting guest comfort or safety (broken AC, no hot water, safety concerns)
  • Medium Priority: Important but not urgent (extra towels, room service requests)
  • Low Priority: Routine tasks (general cleaning, restocking)

Q: How do I send a message to just one guest?

A: Go to Conversations ‚Üí Active, find their conversation, and type your message. Don't use Broadcast for single guests.

Q: What if I send a broadcast message to the wrong guests?

A: You cannot unsend messages. Always use the "Preview" button before sending to check how many guests will receive it. If you make a mistake, send a follow-up clarification message.

Q: Can guests see my name when I reply?

A: Yes, your name or role (e.g., "Front Desk Staff") appears with your messages. Always be professional and courteous.

Q: How do I know if a task is completed?

A: In the Tasks section, completed tasks show a green checkmark or move to the "Completed" column. You can also filter to show only completed tasks.

Q: What if I forget my password?

A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen, or contact your hotel's IT administrator or supervisor to reset it.

Q: Can I use StayBot on my phone?

A: Yes! Open your phone's web browser and go to the same URL. The system works on mobile devices.

Q: How do I print a report?

A: Go to any report page (Analytics, Tasks, Guests), click the "Export" or "Print" button, choose your format (PDF is best for printing), and then print from your browser.

Troubleshooting

Problem: Page is blank or won't load

Solution:

  • Refresh the page (press F5 or click the refresh button)
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try logging out and logging back in
  • Clear your browser cache (ask IT if you don't know how)

Problem: Can't find a conversation or task

Solution:

  • Use the search bar at the top of the page
  • Check if you have filters applied (Status, Date, Department) - clear them to see everything
  • Look in "History" or "Completed" sections - it might have been resolved

Problem: Button won't click or form won't save

Solution:

  • Make sure all required fields (marked with *) are filled in
  • Check for error messages in red text
  • Wait a few seconds - the system might be processing
  • Refresh the page and try again

Problem: Guest says they didn't receive my message

Solution:

  • Check Broadcast ‚Üí History to confirm it was sent
  • Verify you selected the correct audience filters
  • Guest might need to check their WhatsApp or messaging app
  • Send a direct message through Conversations as a backup

Still Having Problems?

Contact your supervisor or IT support team. Have this information ready:

  • What you were trying to do
  • What happened instead
  • Any error messages you saw
  • Your username (not password!)

15. Common Tasks (Step-by-Step Examples)

15.1 Example: Guest Requests Extra Towels

Scenario: A guest in Room 305 messages asking for extra towels.

What to do:
  1. Click Conversations ‚Üí Active in the sidebar
  2. Find the message from Room 305 in the list
  3. Click on the conversation to open it
  4. Read the guest's request
  5. Click Tasks ‚Üí All in the sidebar
  6. Click the "Create Task" button
  7. Fill in:
    • Title: "Extra towels for Room 305"
    • Description: "Guest requested 2 extra bath towels"
    • Department: Housekeeping
    • Priority: Medium
    • Assign to: Select a housekeeping staff member
  8. Click "Save"
  9. Go back to the conversation and reply: "We're sending extra towels to your room right away!"

15.2 Example: Send Welcome Message to New Arrivals

Scenario: It's 2 PM and you want to welcome all guests checking in today.

What to do:
  1. Click Broadcast ‚Üí Compose in the sidebar
  2. Click "Select Template" dropdown
  3. Choose "Welcome Message" template
  4. Review the message - it should say something like:
    "Welcome to [Hotel Name]! We're delighted to have you. If you need anything, just message us here. Enjoy your stay!"
  5. Under "Select Audience", choose:
    • Filter by: Check-in Date
    • Date: Today
  6. Click "Preview" to see how many guests will receive it
  7. Click "Send Now"
  8. You'll see a confirmation: "Message sent to 12 guests"

15.3 Example: Room 201 Has a Broken AC

Scenario: Guest in Room 201 reports the air conditioning isn't working.

What to do:
  1. Click Tasks ‚Üí All in the sidebar
  2. Click "Create Task"
  3. Fill in:
    • Title: "AC not working - Room 201"
    • Description: "Guest reports AC not cooling. Needs immediate attention."
    • Department: Maintenance
    • Priority: High (because it affects guest comfort)
    • Due Date: Today
    • Assign to: Select maintenance technician
  4. Click "Save"
  5. The maintenance team will see this task immediately
  6. Go to Conversations and message the guest: "We've notified maintenance and they'll be there within 30 minutes to fix your AC."

15.4 Example: Add New Room Service Item

Scenario: Your hotel now offers a "Late Night Snack Box" service.

What to do:
  1. Click Configuration ‚Üí Services in the sidebar
  2. Click "Add New" button at the top
  3. Fill in the form:
    • Service Name: "Late Night Snack Box"
    • Description: "Assorted snacks and beverages delivered to your room"
    • Category: Room Service
    • Price: $15.00
    • Available Hours: 10 PM - 2 AM
    • Status: Active
  4. Click "Save"
  5. The service now appears in the guest menu and they can order it through the chat

15.5 Example: Check Today's Check-ins

Scenario: It's morning and you want to see who's arriving today.

What to do:
  1. Click Guests ‚Üí Check-ins in the sidebar
  2. You'll see a list of all guests checking in today with:
    • Guest name
    • Room number
    • Expected arrival time
    • Booking status
  3. Click on any guest to see full booking details
  4. Use this list to prepare rooms and plan staffing
  5. Click "Export" to print a copy for the front desk

15.6 Example: Guest Asks About Pool Hours

Scenario: Guest messages asking "What time does the pool close?"

What to do:
  1. Click Conversations ‚Üí Active
  2. Find and open the guest's conversation
  3. Option 1 - Quick Reply:
    • Type your response: "The pool is open from 6 AM to 10 PM daily. Enjoy!"
    • Click "Send"
  4. Option 2 - Use FAQ (if available):
    • Click "Quick Replies" or "FAQ" button
    • Select "Pool Hours" from the list
    • The pre-written answer is automatically sent

15.7 Example: View This Week's Performance

Scenario: Your manager asks for this week's guest satisfaction scores.

What to do:
  1. Click Reports ‚Üí Analytics in the sidebar
  2. At the top, click the date range selector
  3. Click "This Week"
  4. Scroll down to find the "Guest Satisfaction" card
  5. You'll see:
    • Average satisfaction score (e.g., 4.5 out of 5)
    • Number of responses
    • Trend compared to last week
  6. Click "Export" to download a detailed report
  7. Choose "PDF" to share with your manager

15.8 Example: Emergency - Fire Alarm Test

Scenario: Fire alarm test scheduled at 2 PM. Need to notify all guests.

What to do:
  1. Click Broadcast ‚Üí Emergency in the sidebar
  2. Select "Fire Alarm Test" template (or write your own)
  3. Message example:
    "NOTICE: Fire alarm test will occur at 2:00 PM today. The alarm will sound for approximately 5 minutes. This is only a test. No action required."
  4. Under audience, select "All Current Guests"
  5. Click "Send Immediately"
  6. All guests receive the notification instantly
  7. Check Broadcast ‚Üí History to confirm delivery
